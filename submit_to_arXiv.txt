You want to submit your article to arXiv?

Submission Guidelines:
  https://info.arxiv.org/help/submit/index.html

Steps:

First time - register (create account) on arXiv.org
   (email, username, password, ...)
You may need an endorsement:
   https://info.arxiv.org/help/endorsement.html


Follow arXiv's guidelines for formatting your paper
(TeX/LaTeX or PDF)
For example, use Google Docs to create your article,
then download it as PDF:
   https://info.arxiv.org/help/submit_pdf.html

Log in to your arXiv account
Select Submit
Fill out the necessary metadata
   
   (title, authors, abstract, and subject classification)

Upload your PDF file and any supplemental material

Select the appropriate subject area for your paper

Click Submit
Select Arxiv.org
Download the zip file with submission files
Upload the zip file to arXiv

Researchers often submit papers to arXiv after submitting 
them to a journal for publication or after they have 
been accepted for publication. 

All members must comply with arXiv's Code of Conduct. 
   https://info.arxiv.org/help/policies/code_of_conduct.html
